Our Services

A Detailed Overview of Our Service Portfolio

Serbacs offers integrated, multidisciplinary business solutions designed to support organizations across their entire growth journey.

1. Business Management Consulting

Strategic financial and operational advisory to strengthen core business functions.

  • CFO & Finance Controller Services: Leadership level financial insights, MIS review.
  • Budgeting & Forecasting: Rolling projections, variance reporting, cash flow planning.
  • Feasibility Studies: Market evaluation, ROI, and break-even studies.
  • Internal Controls & SOP: Risk-based frameworks, workflow design, optimisation.
  • Costing & Performance: Pricing strategy, KPI dashboards, business intelligence.

2. Accounting & Reporting

Accurate, compliant, and timely accounting for financial integrity and operational clarity.

  • General Accounting & Bookkeeping
  • Financial Analysis (cost, revenue, variance insights)
  • MIS Reporting & Management Dashboards
  • Preparation of Financial Statements (BS, P&L, Cash Flow)
  • Receivables & Payables Management & Audit Support

3. Banking Process

Handling banking coordination for seamless transaction flow and adherence to regulations.

  • Coordination for outward/inward remittances
  • Ad-hoc compliance (KYC, queries, certifications)

4. Tax Consulting

Direct Tax (Income Tax)
  • Tax Planning & Advisory
  • ITR Filing (ITR 1 to ITR 7)
  • TDS/TCS Returns & Reconciliations
  • Assessment Representation
Indirect Tax (GST)
  • Registration & Amendments
  • Monthly/Quarterly/Annual Returns
  • LUT Filing & E-Invoicing
  • GST Audit & Notice Handling

5. New Business Formation

Setting up business structures: Pvt Ltd, Public Ltd, OPC, LLP, Partnership, Sole Proprietorship, Section 8.

6. Subsidiary Formation

Helping international companies establish an Indian presence: Wholly Owned Subsidiary, Branch/Liaison Office, FEMA, RBI, MCA compliance.

7. Entity Restructuring

Transitioning to effective structures: Co to LLP, LLP to Co, Entity migration planning & compliance.

8. Legal Consulting

  • Corporate Legal Advisory
  • Agreement Drafting & Review
  • Labour Law Compliance
  • General Legal Documentation Support

9. Compliance Handling

  • MCA/ROC Annual & Event based Compliance
  • GST & Income Tax Compliance
  • Labour Law Compliance
  • Sector/Industry specific Compliance
