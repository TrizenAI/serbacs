Strategic Entity Transformation

As your business grows, your legal and operational structure must evolve. Serbacs assists in choosing and transitioning to the most effective business structure for tax efficiency, compliance ease, and long-term expansion.

Restructuring Options

Company → LLP

Transitioning from a Private Limited Company to a Limited Liability Partnership (LLP) for operational flexibility and reduced tax surcharge.

LLP → Private Limited

Scaling up from an LLP to a Private Limited Company to attract equity investment and institutional funding.

Firm → Company

Converting traditional Partnership Firms into Private Limited Companies to ensure perpetual succession and separate legal identity.

Migration Planning

Complete planning and compliance management for entity migration, office shifts, and structural transitions.

Post-Restructuring Compliance

Ensuring all legal titles, GST registrations, and professional licences are updated seamlessly following the transition.
