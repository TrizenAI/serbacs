About Serbacs

Study → Strategy → Success

Serbacs India Private Limited is a multidisciplinary business advisory and consulting firm delivering end to end solutions across finance, accounting, taxation, legal, compliance, restructuring, and business formation.

Our work is guided by the philosophy: Study → Strategy → Success. We begin with in depth understanding (Study), design practical and growth oriented solutions (Strategy), and execute with precision to achieve measurable results (Success).

With a strong foundation in finance, corporate compliance, taxation, and legal frameworks, Serbacs supports businesses across their entire lifecycle—from formation and expansion to transformation and governance.

We partner with startups, SMEs, corporates, and international companies setting up operations in India, ensuring seamless business operations with complete regulatory confidence.
